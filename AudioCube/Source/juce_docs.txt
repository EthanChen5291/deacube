JUCE::COMPONENT

resized()	Called whenever the window changes size. This is where you calculate where your 144 squares should be.

mouseDrag()	Like mouseDown, but triggers while you move the mouse. Perfect for "painting" colors onto your grid!

mouseEnter()	Triggers when the mouse hovers over the grid. Good for showing a "highlight" square.

setBounds()	How you tell a component where to sit on the screen and how big to be.

isVisible()	A simple check to see if the component is currently hidden.

g.drawRect(x, y, w, h, thickness): Draws just the outline/border.

g.setOpacity(float): Makes the next thing you draw see-through (0.0 to 1.0).
