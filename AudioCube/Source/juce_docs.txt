JUCE::COMPONENT

resized()	Called whenever the window changes size. This is where you calculate where your 144 squares should be.

mouseDrag()	Like mouseDown, but triggers while you move the mouse. Perfect for "painting" colors onto your grid!

mouseEnter()	Triggers when the mouse hovers over the grid. Good for showing a "highlight" square.

setBounds()	How you tell a component where to sit on the screen and how big to be.

isVisible()	A simple check to see if the component is currently hidden.

JUCE Component

addAndMakeVisible(child): The "Adoption." It tells the Window: 
                        "This grid is now your child. Please draw it and pass 
                        mouse clicks to it."

setBounds(x, y, w, h)	The "Floor Plan." Without this, the child exists but has 
                        a size of 0x0. You usually call this inside resized().

removeFromDesktop(): The "Delete." Useful if you want a window to vanish.

repaint()	The "Refresh." You call this when a variable changes (like a tile color) 
            and you want the screen to update.

______

bufferToFill.clearActiveBufferRegion()

Wipes the sandbox clean (sets everything to 0.0). 
If you forget this, you might hear a "buzzing" sound from the previous 
block of audio that was sitting in that RAM.

bufferToFill.numSamples

This is the Size of the sandbox. It changes depending on the user's sound settings, 
so never hardcode "512"—always use this variable.

shutdownAudio()

You call this in the Destructor. It tells the Sound Card: "Stop yelling at me for numbers, 
I'm going to sleep." If you don't call this, your app might crash when you close it.
